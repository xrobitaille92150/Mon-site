# Business Overview — Expert Zone Advisory

## 1\. Company Identity

**Structure:** Solo freelance consultant, operating under the brand **Expert Zone Advisory**.

**What the business does:** Supports insurers, asset managers and finance functions on complex issues at the intersection of finance, accounting, reporting, investments, operations and systems.

**Client archetypes (three distinct profiles):**

- **Vendor side:** Implementation or customer satisfaction directors at software/consulting vendors  
- **End-client side:** CFO, Chief Accounting Officer, Chief Investment Officer, Finance Transformation Manager, Programme Manager  
- **Consulting firm side:** Partners who bring Xavier in as a specialist subcontractor

**One-sentence positioning:** A multi-specialist covering the full spectrum of what makes insurance and asset management technically distinct — from regulatory accounting to investment reporting to programme delivery.

---

## 2\. Revenue Model

**Current monthly revenue:** \~€20,000 (20 billable days × €1,000/day)

**Revenue streams:**

- **Core consulting / interim management:** 100% of active revenue  
- **Freelancer coaching:** Material in 2022–2023; not currently active  
- **Freelancer placement fees:** Occasional and opportunistic; not actively pursued

**Current situation:** One active assignment (started 18 months ago), ending May 2026\. Transition to next assignment is imminent and urgent.

**Trajectory:** Flat. Daily rate stable at €1,000/day for several years, with slight downward pressure. Previously billed above €1,200/day; such opportunities have become rarer due to structural market compression (intermediary margin, client pushback, increased competition).

**Growth lever identified:** Full remote positioning would allow simultaneous multi-client engagements and materially increase turnover without increasing the daily rate.

---

## 3\. Offer Stack

**Single active offer: Senior Consulting / Interim Management**

- **Rate:** €1,000/day (standard); higher rates achievable on rare, highly specialized mandates (PMO leadership, niche regulatory/reporting work)  
- **Typical duration:** Long-term assignments (12–24+ months)  
- **Scope:** Varies by assignment — PMO/governance, hands-on interim management, technical accounting, investment reporting, regulatory transformation (IFRS 17, IFRS 9, Solvency II), system implementation support (incl. SimCorp-related)  
- **Pricing factors:** Specialization rarity, client size, assignment duration, presence of intermediary

**Inactive / opportunistic offers:**

- **Freelancer coaching:** Targeted at professionals transitioning to freelance consulting. Was priced and marketed in 2022–2023 but not currently active. Pre-AI economics made it margin-negative after marketing costs.  
- **Placement / referral:** Occasional only. Negative past experiences with subcontractor quality have deprioritized this stream.

---

## 4\. Team & Operations

**Team:** None. Solo operation, by deliberate choice.

**Core tools:** Microsoft Office suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint). No specialist CRM, project management, or automation stack currently in place.

**Key operational bottleneck:** Non-billable time lost to low-value activities — unnecessary meetings, slide deck preparation, meeting minutes. Prime target for AI-driven automation.

---

## 5\. Market Position

**Differentiation:** Genuinely rare profile combining:

- Top-tier education: ESCP (Top 2 French business school, European Tier 1\) \+ actuarial studies \+ accounting qualification  
- 25 years of insurance-specific experience from day one of career  
- Dual coverage: business/finance side AND investment/asset management side  
- Delivery range: both PMO/governance leadership and hands-on interim management  
- Bilingual: French and English; open to international mandates

**Competitive moat:** The combination of actuarial, accounting, ESCP, insurance specialization from day one, and cross-functional delivery breadth is rare enough to be a structural differentiator — not a marginal one.

**Pricing position:** Mid-to-premium. Currently at €1,000/day; formerly above €1,200/day on selective mandates. Rate compression is market-driven, not a reflection of value delivered.

**Typical time-to-placement:** 2 weeks to 1 month from active search to signed engagement.
